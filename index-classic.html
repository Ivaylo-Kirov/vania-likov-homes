About Vania

Born and raised in Sofia, Bulgaria, Vania graduated with honors from the University of Sofia in both Psychology and Music. Upon arriving in Canada, she leveraged her ability to speak 5 languages and her desire to help people to build a successful real estate career.

Since 1994, Vania has helped hundreds of families navigate the Toronto real estate market. Her uncompromising integrity, strong work ethic, and enthusiasm consistently result in win-win outcomes for her clients.

When not helping clients, Vania volunteers at the Toronto Humane Society and holds a 3rd degree black belt in Karate.
